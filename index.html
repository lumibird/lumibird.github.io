About RADIANT GROUP

RADIANT GROUP is a study group at Kyonggi University that studies computer science and develops programs for a better world. Anyone who likes change can join and we are ready to welcome you at any time. Work with us to realize your brilliant ideas and face a brilliant future. Now all you have to do is be part of us.

RADIANTERS

RADIANTER means a member who works in our organization. It means a radiant person by adding the adjective "radiant" and the suffix "-er" that means a person.

Our member is looking for someone who enjoys focusing on something and has a passionate attitude. Don't hesitate to contact me if you think so. We are always waiting.
